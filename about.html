What is Mardi Gras?

Mardi Gras is one of the most widely known Carnival celebrations in the world. It is a vibrant and colorful celebration that takes place in New Orleans, Louisiana each year. Mardi Gras is a French term that translates to Fat Tuesday, and is celebrated the day before Lent begins. The celebration was first started by French settlers in the 1700s. It has become an iconic event in New Orleans, and people from around the world come to the city to experience it.

The celebration of Mardi Gras consists of parades, carnival games, and live music. There are dozens of parades that take place throughout the city, which feature colorful floats and marching bands. People in the parades often wear elaborate costumes and throw colorful beads and trinkets to the crowd. There are also several street festivals and events hosted throughout the city during Mardi Gras. It is a time of celebration, and the people of New Orleans proudly show off their culture and traditions

New Orlean's Mayor

LaToya Cantrell is the first female mayor of New Orleans, Louisiana, who was elected in 2018. She was born and raised in New Orleans and has spent most of her life in the city. After attending Xavier University of Louisiana, she worked as a non-profit director for 15 years and also served as a City Council Member for three terms.

Mayor Cantrell has worked to improve the city of New Orleans since taking office, focusing on improving public safety, creating a more equitable economy and providing access to quality healthcare. She has also pushed for initiatives such as the creation of a youth employment program, the renovation of public housing, the expansion of public transportation, and the development of a stormwater management plan. Mayor Cantrell has also been working toward a more sustainable future for the city, focusing on renewable energy, coastal restoration, and green infrastructure.

Mayor Cantrell’s focus on the needs of the people of New Orleans has earned her the respect of the community. She has been praised for her commitment to public service, her passion for the city and her dedication to making New Orleans a better place to live. Mayor Cantrell is a driving force behind the city’s continued progress and the hope of a brighter future for the city.
