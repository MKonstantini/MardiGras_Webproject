FAQ

Mardi Gras in New Orleans is on Tuesday, February 16, 2021.

Mardi Gras in New Orleans is a two-week festival, beginning on the Friday before Fat Tuesday, also known as Lundi Gras.

The traditional Mardi Gras colors are purple, green, and gold.

The best place to watch the Mardi Gras parade is along the parade route along St. Charles Avenue.

The most popular food served during Mardi Gras is King Cake, a round cake with a small plastic baby inside.
